SOUF SOUF

Souf Souf is the sound of the youth living in south Seattle with the contracting styles from the duo; G-Baby providing an unforgettable melodic flow paired with Shotta Pistol rhymes that showcase a life unlike most


These two have been providing neighborhood hit after hit with consistency, cumulating over 100k Views on “Big Flip” and over 50k on “Sinner” via Youtube.


Attracting comparisons from current hip hop acts such as A Boogie and Juice World, The unsung heroes have established them as the Seattle’s next mainstream artists.


In 2018, he signed to Jay Park’s H1GHR MUSIC where he released two well-received projects; Darko and Truly Unruly. Best known for his standout verses on tracks like “WDFA” with Jay Park and Higher Brothers, and “Comrades” featuring Pressa, 28AV has showcased his unique cultural background in collaborations with artists from all around the world. Over the years he has shared the stage with hip-hop icons including Young Jeezy, Pusha T, and the late Nipsey Hussle, and has collaborated with Lil Wayne, Max B, and many more..


Souf Souf는 Seattle 남부에서 다양하고 유니크한 스타일과 사운드로 유명세를 타기 시작한 듀오 아티스트이다.


G-Baby는 한번 들으면 잊을 수 없는 멜로디컬한 플로우가 돋보이는 멤버이며, 또 다른 멤버인 Shotta Pistol은 여태 다른 곳에서는 들을 수 없었던 색다른 라임을 보여준다.


이 두 사람은 유튜브를 통해 ‘Big Flip’으로 10만회 이상, ‘Sinner’로 5만회 이상의 조회 수를 기록하며,


A Boogie, Juice World와 같이 Seattle의 차세대 주류 아티스트로서 성장해 갈 힙합 듀오로 많은 팬들의 기대를 한 몸에 받고 있다.
